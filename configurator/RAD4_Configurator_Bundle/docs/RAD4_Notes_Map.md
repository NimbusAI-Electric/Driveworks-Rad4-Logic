# 1. Categories you asked about

## SPECIFICATION

The **SPECIFICATION** block is the installation prose on page 1 of every sales aid. Variants differ by cable type (MC vs plain), whip length (15" / 18" / 30" / 36"), whether 0-10V or low-voltage control wires are called out, and whether mounting is described as "fixture", "driver enclosure", or "mirror".
